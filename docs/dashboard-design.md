# Dashboard Design

## Objective

To provide a comprehensive view of business performance across sales, customers, and logistics.

## Pages Description

### Executive Overview

High-level KPIs and trends for quick decision-making.

### Sales & Profitability

Focus on revenue drivers, product performance, and discount impact.

### Customer Insights

Analysis of customer behavior, segmentation, and retention.

### Logistics & Operations

Evaluation of shipping performance and operational efficiency.

## Design Principles

* Clear hierarchy of information
* Consistent color usage
* KPI-driven layout
* Interactive filtering

## Key Decisions

* Use of KPIs for executive clarity
* Segmentation by region and category
* Inclusion of operational metrics (logistics)
